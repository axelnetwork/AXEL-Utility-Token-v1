3.2. Pending Transactions. A Digital Currency Transfer is not complete while it is in a pending state. Pending Digital Currency Transfers that are initiated from an AXEL Wallet will reflect a pending transaction status and are not available to you for use on the AXEL.Network or otherwise while the transaction is pending.
